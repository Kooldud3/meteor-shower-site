What Are Meteors?

Meteors, commonly known as "shooting stars," are small fragments of comets or asteroids that burn up upon entering the Earth's atmosphere. Every year, there are several predictable meteor showers that light up the night sky.

Meteor Shower Calendar

Plan ahead for the best meteor showers of the year. The next major meteor shower is the Geminids, peaking on December 13-14. Below is a list of some popular upcoming showers:

  • Orionids: October 21-22
  • Leonids: November 16-17
  • Geminids: December 13-14

For a complete calendar, visit the International Meteor Organization Calendar.
